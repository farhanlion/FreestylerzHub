What is Freestle Football?

Freestyle Football is the art of self-expression through creatively juggling a football using any parts of the body, excluding the elbows to the hands. It combines football tricks, dance, acrobatics and music to entertain onlookers and compete with opponents. Browse around the website to discover more about this amazing sport!
